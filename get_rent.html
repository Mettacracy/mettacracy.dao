When Mettacracy members perform any activity within the platform we are making an investment of our time, we place our trust, we contribute our knowledge, talents or experience. Generally speaking, we are adding a unique "Value" to achieve a collective goal. Mettacracy rewards our work by transferring Yma tokens to the wallets of members who hold a position or have staking deposits. Members can then go to exchange their Ymas in the Liquidity Pools for ETH, BTC, TETHER, etc.

All members have the right to participate in any work team, if they qualify for the position in question, they must make their presentation to the DAO or the verification team. The positions or activities that we can perform in Mettacracy are:

Promotion team

Promoters are members who offer technical assistance on their own initiative.

The project also provides support to communities in conflict situations, protestant groups, activist associations, promoters take the necessary first steps to form the necessary communities, organizes special membership registration days, coordinates mentoring sessions to train new members and moderates discussions in the communities with which it collaborates.

Communication is exercised by members who are dedicated to providing content related to decisions made in Mettacracy to influencers in social networks, blogs, messaging groups, digital media, mailing lists, traditional media, public and private institutions, etc.

Communication team

Registration team

Team formed by members in charge of completing the registration of new members through an interview, as a security rule, registrars can only complete 25 registrations and must wait one year to return to the position.

They are members chosen by the participants of a debate to give order to the debate, to guide the participants, to keep the rules of the debate in mind at all times and to mediate between participants in conflict.

Debate Moderation team

Verification team

They are members who verify that all members are real people and qualify applicants to join a work team.

It brings together members who organize courses, tutorials and video tutorials to help other members use the platform and train team members.

Team of Instructors

Team of Consultants

If you are a professional or specialist in an area of expertise, you can serve as a consultant and provide technical assistance to discussion groups, courts, treasury committees or other members who require your services.
