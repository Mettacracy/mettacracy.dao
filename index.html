What is Mettacracy?

Mettacracy is a web 3.0 protocol, a DApp accessible from any device connected to the Internet and allows you to easily expose your ideas to global communities as well as to communities that you can create yourself. If your idea or proposal is supported by the majority, Mettacracy rewards your initiative by transferring IMA tokens to your wallet and a team of thousands of communicators spread the result of your proposal around the world.

With Mettacracy any community can come together and act in consensus to achieve their goals, now we can transmit our discontent and demand answers without the risk of having to make public street demonstrations, without injuries, without material losses and preserving the lives of our comrades.

Through Mettacracy we can organize, unite, cooperate and act as a unit to respond to institutions, organizations and opinion matrixes that pretend to ignore our authority.

How does Mettacracy work?

If you have an idea to propose, if you disagree with the prevailing system, if your representatives are not fighting for your interests, if you don't trust politicians and want to take action to build a fair system for all, your place is in Mettacracy, become a member, add your intelligence and your will to thousands of people just like you.

Political actions begin as a complaint, a nonconformity, a certainty that there are better ways to achieve goals. Mettacracy's objective is to allow any person to initiate a political action by presenting clear proposals to the corresponding community and among all to develop that proposal to adopt it as a solution.

Mettacracy does not demand solutions from any institution, organization or level of government, Mettacracy is the organized society presenting the best solutions and alternatives for all.

Here is a step-by-step explanation of how the protocol works.

Presentation

Mettacracy is designed to make it very easy to present proposed solutions to your complaints or conflict situations.

You fill out a form and your proposal is presented to the community(ies) of your choice.

Debate

A good proposal will attract many members who will support you and work with you to analyze, discuss and develop your proposal.

Together we will study the proposal and how to make it work for the benefit of all.

Voting

At the end of this process the proposal will be presented to the rest of the community for the members to support or reject.

If the proposal is approved, it becomes a majority resolution and is sent to the communications team.

Disclosure

Mettacracy's team of communicators receives instructions and disseminates the content of the resolution by directing communications to those responsible for the conflict and to any entity capable of influencing the resolution of the conflict. The resolution is also made public through digital media, blogs, social networks, messaging app groups, newsletters and mailing lists.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mettacracy a political party or movement?

Mettacracy is not identified with any party, movement or political ideology, it is a platform for free and decentralized participation, a tool to share ideas and proposals, an opportunity to connect with other like-minded people.
Mettacracy is made up of those of us who until now have ceded our power to the politicians.

Who does Mettcracy belong to?

Mettacracy is a DAO, an autonomous and decentralized organization, owned by each registered member, where everyone has equal power and voting rights regardless of the number of tokens owned.

Who runs Mettacracy?

The platform is a horizontal and democratic organization, apart from the basic rules, decisions are made in voting processes and then taken to automatically executed smart contracts. There are no leaders, directors, managers or subordinates, we organize ourselves in work teams and other internal DAO's to develop proposals that require specialized knowledge.

What power does Mettacracy have to impose solutions?

Mettacracy is a protocol to help a collective to reach consensus, it never imposes solutions, it only shows the world the agreements reached by the majority of its members and each human being can choose to join the agreement or ignore it.

Is Mettacracy a social network?

It is not a social network, the activity within the platform is aimed at presenting ideas, to debate them and after obtaining the support of the majority, to show them to the world. It is therefore a network of decision makers.

Is what is done at Mettacracy legal?

On the platform, members share their ideas and present them to the majority in order to achieve consensus while preserving values such as respect and peace. Violent actions and conflict solutions or the use of force are never promoted.
